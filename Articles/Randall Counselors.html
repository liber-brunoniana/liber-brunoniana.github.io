Randall Counselors

Categories

  • Articles

    • The Randall Counselors program which began in 1976 revived the policy of personal counseling for individual students which had been the practice of former dean Otis E. Randall for whom the program was named. The program, designed to combine advising and counseling, was later aimed at sophomores and manned by faculty members who served two-year terms.
