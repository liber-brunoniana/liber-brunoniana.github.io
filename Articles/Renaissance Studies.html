    • Renaissance Studies is an interdisciplinary concentration sponsored by the departments of Art, Music, History, English, French Studies, Hispanic and Italian Studies, Comparative Literature, German, Classics, and History of Mathematics. Chairpersons of the program have been Allen S. Trueblood, Barbara K. Lewalski, Andrew J. Sabol, Walter R. Davis, Karen Newman, Anthony Mohlo, and Coppelia Kahn.
