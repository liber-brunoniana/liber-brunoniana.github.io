    • Los, subtitled “a magazine of poetry,” was published by the graduate students of Brown University from March 1967 to the spring of 1970. The magazine was edited by Laurence Goldstein, and included the work of Barton St. Armand ’65, Edwin Honig, Keith Waldrop, and Rosemarie Waldrop, and illustrations by John Udvardy and from the The Psalm Book of Charles Knowles, the work of Charles Knowles ’61, who died in his freshman year at Brown.
